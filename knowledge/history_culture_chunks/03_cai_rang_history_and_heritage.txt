CHUNK 03 - CAI RANG HISTORY AND HERITAGE

Noi dung chinh:
Cho noi Cai Rang hinh thanh tu boi canh giao thong duong thuy tung giu vai tro chu dao o Dong bang song Cuu Long. Day la cho dau moi nong san tren song, noi mua ban rau cu trai cay voi nhip cho som tinh mo sang.

Diem van hoa noi bat:
- Hinh thuc "treo beo" de gioi thieu mat hang la dau an van hoa thuong ho dac trung.
- Nhip sinh hoat cua gia dinh thuong ho tren ghe tau la mot phan cua di san doi song song nuoc.
- Cho duoc cong nhan la Di san van hoa phi vat the cap quoc gia (nam 2016).

Y nghia voi website Go Quest:
Go Quest co he sinh thai diem den Can Tho, trong do Cai Rang dai dien cho "nen van hoa song nuoc truyen thong". Khi user hoi ve lich su du lich Can Tho, nen uu tien Cai Rang nhu minh chung song dong ve su chuyen doi tu thuong mai duong thuy sang du lich van hoa.

Thong tin co the tra loi nhanh:
- Gio cho nhon nhip nhat: khoang 4h-6h sang, tham quan dep nhat thuong 5h-7h.
- Ban sac: mua ban tren ghe, am thuc sang tren song, doi song thuong ho.
- Vai tro hien nay: vua la khong gian giao thuong, vua la tai san van hoa va du lich.
