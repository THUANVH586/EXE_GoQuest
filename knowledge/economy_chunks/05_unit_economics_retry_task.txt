CHUNK 05 - UNIT ECONOMICS CHO RETRY NHIEM VU

Don vi phan tich de xuat: 1 lan retry nhiem vu.

Doanh thu don vi:
- Gia ban hien thi: 0.5 USD / lan retry.

Chi phi bien co the gan voi 1 lan retry:
- Phi thanh toan cong giao dich.
- Chi phi ho tro khach neu phat sinh loi giao dich.
- Chi phi van hanh bo sung neu retry tao tang tai cho staff.

Bien loi nhuan don vi (khung tu duy):
- Unit Margin = 0.5 USD - (Payment Fee + Support Cost + Variable Ops Cost)

Diem can theo doi de toi uu:
- Ty le nhiem vu het han tren tong so nhiem vu da bat dau.
- Ty le khach chap nhan retry sau khi het han.
- So lan retry trung binh moi user.
- Ty le hoan tat sau retry (neu cao thi retry co gia tri that).

Ham y kinh te:
- Retry la nguon thu bo sung va la co che dieu tiet hanh vi.
- Neu retry qua cao co the gay bo cuoc; neu retry qua thap co the khong du bu chi phi.
- Muc 0.5 USD dang dinh vi theo huong de tiep can, phu hop trai nghiem dai chung.
