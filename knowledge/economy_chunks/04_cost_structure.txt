CHUNK 04 - COST STRUCTURE (CAU TRUC CHI PHI)

Nhom chi phi van hanh chinh:
1) Chi phi van hanh trai nghiem tai diem
- Nhan su staff tai cho.
- Dieu phoi hoat dong xac nhan nhiem vu.
- Ho tro khach trong qua trinh trai nghiem.

2) Chi phi qua tang doi diem
- Gia tri qua tang vat ly.
- Chi phi ton kho va bo sung ton.
- Rui ro ton kho het han/khong quay vong nhanh.

3) Chi phi marketing va tang truong
- Thu hut khach moi.
- Noi dung thuong hieu du lich xanh va cong dong.
- Hop tac voi kenh du lich dia phuong.

4) Chi phi thanh toan va doi soat
- Phi giao dich thanh toan cho retry.
- Chi phi doi soat va xu ly giao dich thanh cong/that bai.

5) Chi phi quan tri chat luong
- Dam bao trai nghiem dong deu giua cac staff va ca nhiem vu.
- Xu ly khieu nai va ho tro sau trai nghiem.

Goc nhin kinh te:
- Loi nhuan phu thuoc vao can bang giua doanh thu retry + doanh thu bo sung va tong chi phi van hanh + qua tang + marketing.
